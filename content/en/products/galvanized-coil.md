Galvanized steel coils are coated with a layer of zinc to protect against corrosion. These products are widely used in construction, roofing, and automotive industries.

## Specifications

- **Thickness**: 0.3mm – 3.0mm
- **Width**: 600mm – 1500mm
- **Coating**: Z100 – Z275
- **Standard**: EN 10346
