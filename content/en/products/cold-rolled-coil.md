Cold Rolled Coils (CRC) offer superior surface quality and tighter dimensional tolerances compared to hot-rolled products. Ideal for applications requiring a smooth finish and precise thickness control.

## Specifications

- **Thickness**: 0.3mm – 3.0mm
- **Width**: 600mm – 1500mm
- **Grade**: DC01, DC03, DC04
- **Standard**: EN 10130
